━━━ SEQUENCE RULES ━━━

- duration_bars: how long each step plays (1 bar = 4 beats). Default: 4.
- repeats: how many times the full step list plays before advancing to the next sequence. Default: 1.
- Sequences switch at 16-beat (4-bar) phrase boundaries — clean DJ-style cuts.
- A held sequence freezes on its last frame until the next phrase boundary arrives.
- Steps within a sequence play in order and loop automatically for `repeats` cycles.

━━━ FILTERS ━━━

Applied in order, outermost last:
- gamma:   gamma (1.0-3.0) — corrects LED brightness curve; 2.2 is standard
- mirror:  no params — symmetric reflection from center outward
- reverse: no params — flip LED direction
- dim:     brightness (0.0-1.0) — overall brightness reduction
